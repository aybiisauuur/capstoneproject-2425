Live Chat

The Live Chat allows for two-way communication between the hearing and deaf individuals.

Please allow the deaf client to scan this code for the chat to appear on their end.

REPLY BACK

You may use this feature to send a response back to the deaf client
(if modules do not have a SL equivalent of the response you are trying to communicate).
Please reply back in English, and keep your responses short and simple.
As much as possible, do limit the use of this feature and use sign language to communicate.
